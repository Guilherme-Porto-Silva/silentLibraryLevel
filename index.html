The Comfortable Rooms

Comfortable Rooms are cubes with four doors, one at each side, soft and new sofas, a table at the very middle with four glass-made squares and dark oak tables scatered around, on wich are found sugar and cookie pots, cafeterias and coolers with almond water. Soft and cute red carpets cover their floors, making it a not bad idea to sit or lie on them insted of the sofas. Those tables at the very middle generate papper-made creations every time a person walks into the Comfortable Room they are in, wich are clearly based on that person, possibly being the one they most like, the one they were willing to find or one that ansers a question that has been noguing their haed in the close past.

The object generated so many papper creations that the entire room was taken over by papper and latter capes, deformating eachother by smashing against walls and roof. Being instantanius the generation process, it is obvius that few of those testers survived, being there where the most interesting part of this history begings: while reading paranormal-themed books, other level visitors, among wich many had no idea of the testing event, started documenting sightings of ghost-like humanoids that mach to the aperences of those dead testers, but never of those few that survived and came to die in other levels.
